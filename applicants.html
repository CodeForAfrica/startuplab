2024 Pre-launch Lab Participants
Over the years, Google News Initiative and Code for Africa have supported African news industry in digital transformation, adapting to changing consumer preferences, and combating misinformation. The 10-week Pre-Launch Lab aims to support independent, early-stage news startups in achieving financial and operational sustainability. Selected from 100+ applicants across ten Kenyan counties, our diverse cohort of 24 startups focuses on high-quality reporting tailored for local communities, niche audiences, and underserved communities. Covering a range of journalism categories such as social impact, youth, gender, fact-checking, and climate, these start-ups are transformative force shaping the future of the news industry in Kenya
