What’s included
The Google News Initiative Pre-launch Lab supports early stage news entrepreneurs. We are excited to bring the program to Kenya in collaboration with Code for Africa.
The pre-launch lab is a ten-week, fully remote programme for early-stage startups. The lab’s mission is to help entrepreneurs prepare to launch successful, trusted digital news organisations. The lab accepts up to 24 solo founders or teams of aspiring entrepreneurs who have identified a community’s need for better news and want guidance and coaching on how to launch, or continue to develop, a news offering that delivers a solution rooted in journalism.
Made for journalism
A laser-focused & immensely practical curriculum that is grounded in hands-on learning and getting things done.
Live seminars
Weekly live video seminars with a small cohort of like-minded journalism entrepreneurs.
Personalized coaching
Weekly one-on-one coaching to help you move your idea forward toward launch, or growth.
Eligibility Criteria
Geographic Eligibility
You must be based in Kenya, and targeting a predominantly Kenyan audience.
You should consider this Pre-launch Lab if:
You’ve been thinking long and hard about launching your own digital news effort
Or you’re less than 2 years into running your news business and want to re-evaluate some of your core hypotheses and make adjustments to improve your financial sustainability
You have identified a community’s need for better news, information and/or engagement and think you can deliver a solution
You're committed to public-service reporting working with and alongside the communities you serve
You’re committed to making your business idea financially sustainable as quickly as possible
and you...
Can commit at least eight hours a week for ten weeks to turn your business idea into reality (ideally, you can commit more time)
Are willing to make time to share insights with other participants
Lastly...
You must plan to be independently owned and operated
You must not plan to be employed by a government entity
You must not be a Google employee or contractor
Applications close on Monday, December 11, 2023.
